-Level 35/144:
Adjacent to the shadows in Liber Primus there's a numeric palindrome that results in 5. What is it?


Solution: We are asked to find a numeric palindrome that results in 5. The first part of the hint says adjacent to the shadows in Liber Primus. We can do some research for what that means.

Link: https://uncovering-cicada.fandom.com/wiki/CICADA_3301_2014_PUZZLE_FACTS_PART_5

We discover a 5 by 5 magic square under the section titled "Some Wisdom". Next to the word shadows, we see the number 131 which is a numeric palindrome. If we sum up the digits, we get the number 5.


Answer: 131
