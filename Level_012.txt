-Level 12/144:
A red line runs through it, third from the right looks like a misspelt laugh. Where am I in the UK?


Solution: We are asked to figure out where we are. We can look up red line in UK and find out that the Central Line in the London Underground is a red line on the map.

Map: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_line_(London_Underground)#Map


Answer: London
