-Level 31/144:
What hit a windshield using Trifid 5 in the 24th March bird song?

Solution: We are asked to figure out what hit a windshield. We recognie that bird song means Twitter. We can go to the 12 rings Twitter and find posts from March 24th.

Link: https://twitter.com/the12rings/status/1242488078654042113

We also recognize that Trifid is a cipher. We can use an online Trifid Decoder.

Link: https://cryptii.com/pipes/trifid-cipher

We can decode the ciphertext using an empty string as key and group size of 5 to get "cicadahitawindshield".

Answer: cicada
