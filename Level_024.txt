-Level 24/144:
Inside the Piperaceae family, you can find an entry pass to a village. Once inside, what will you probably drink?


Solution: We are asked to find the name of a drink. The Piperaceae family is also known as the pepper family. We can use wikipedia to learn more about it.

Link: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Piperaceae

After doing some more research, on villages that use pepper in their drinks, we discover that the Kava plant is often used in drinks for villages throughout the Pacific Islands.

Link: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kava

Specifically, villages in Fiji typically use Kava roots to make a drink called grog.


Answer: grog
