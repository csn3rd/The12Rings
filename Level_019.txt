The Anatidae name of a deep web engine

Solution: We are asked to find the name of a deep web engine. The hint says that it has an Anatidae name. Meaning it has some form of water bird in the name (ducks, geese, swans, etc.).

We can look for deep web engines and find one with Duck in the name. Link: https://duckduckgo.com/
